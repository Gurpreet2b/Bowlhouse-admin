Terms and Conditions

Terms and conditions are a binding set of rules that serve as a contract
between you and the users who visit your website or use your app.
They are also known as terms of service and terms of use, and
are abbreviated as T&Cs (or ToS and ToU, respectively). Having terms and
conditions isn’t a legal requirement, but they are critical to maintaining your rights
and protecting your business. Therefore, it’s in your best interest to create a terms and conditions
template for your services, and display the agreement on your site.
